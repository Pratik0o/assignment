KitKat - Have a Break, Have a KitKat

Introduction

KitKat, a crispy wafer bar covered in smooth chocolate, was first introduced in 1935 by Rowntree's of York, UK. It has become a global sensation with a wide variety of flavors and styles.

Fun Facts

  • Over 300 unique KitKat flavors are available in Japan.
  • The iconic slogan "Have a Break, Have a KitKat" was first used in 1958.
  • KitKat is sold in more than 100 countries worldwide.
